Full Details:


Ground Floor
The property has a spacious entrance porch which is large enough to be used as a seating area allowing you to take in the beautiful countryside views to the front of the property. The front facing lounge provides plenty of space for freestanding furniture and has beautiful high ceilings along with attractive coving. All decorated in modern tones along with fitted laminate flooring the lounge provides a tasteful space in which to relax. The kitchen/breakfasting room presents with tiled flooring and includes an extensive range of wall and base units with contrasting laminate worktops. The kitchen design provides space for freestanding appliances and dining furniture. In addition a useful separate utility/WC room offers space for freestanding plumbed appliance along with a convenient fitted low level WC and washbasin. A good-sized under-stairs cupboard provides additional storage too!

First Floor
The first-floor landing presents access to both bedrooms along with the family bathroom. The bedrooms and landing are all recently carpeted. Both bedrooms overlook the stunning countryside while allowing space for double beds and freestanding furniture. The stylish modern bathroom provides a fitted bathtub, separate shower cubicle, floating washbasin with vanity unit, low level WC and chrome ladder radiator.

Externally
The front of the property is enclosed by a traditional brick wall with metal gated access. The rear of the property is south facing and currently houses a removable garage, this currently isn't included.
